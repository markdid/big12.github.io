Brief Overview:
The Arizona Wildcats football program represents the University of Arizona in American college football. They compete in the Football Bowl Subdivision (FBS) of the NCAA and the South Division of the Pac-12 Conference. Their home games are played at Arizona Stadium in Tucson, Arizona. The team's inaugural season was in 1889. They joined the Pac-10 Conference in 1978 alongside rival Arizona State and became a member of the Pac-12 South Division when the conference realigned in 2011.
